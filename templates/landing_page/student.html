Popular Questions

Answers to common questions from students

How do I choose the right counselor for my needs?

When choosing a counselor, consider their specialization, experience, and approach. You can browse counselor profiles, read reviews from other students, and even schedule initial consultations to find the best fit for your specific needs and goals.

How much does counseling cost?

Counseling costs vary depending on the counselor's experience, specialization, and session duration. We offer a range of options to fit different budgets, including package deals and subscription plans. Some counselors also offer sliding scale fees for students with financial need.

How often should I meet with my counselor?

The frequency of sessions depends on your individual needs and goals. Some students benefit from weekly sessions, while others may meet with their counselor bi-weekly or monthly. Your counselor will work with you to determine the optimal schedule for your specific situation.

Can I change counselors if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, absolutely. We understand that finding the right counselor is important for a successful counseling relationship. If you feel that your current counselor isn't the right fit, you can easily switch to another counselor on our platform at any time.

Are the counseling sessions confidential?

Yes, confidentiality is a cornerstone of our service. All sessions and communications with your counselor are private and secure. Our counselors adhere to strict ethical guidelines and privacy policies to ensure your information remains confidential.
