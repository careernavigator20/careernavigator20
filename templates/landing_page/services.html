Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How do I know which service is right for me?

If you're unsure which service would be most beneficial for your specific situation, we recommend starting with a free consultation. Our team will assess your needs and goals, and recommend the most appropriate services to help you achieve success.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my package?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your package at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. There are no penalties or fees for changing your subscription level.

Are there any discounts for students?

Yes, we offer special student rates for all our services. Simply verify your student status with a valid .edu email address or student ID, and you'll receive a 20% discount on any package.

How quickly can I expect results?

Results vary depending on your specific goals, current situation, and the effort you put into implementing the guidance provided. Some clients see immediate improvements in areas like resume quality or interview performance, while career transitions and long-term goals may take several months to achieve.

Can I request a specific counselor or mentor?

Yes, you can request to work with a specific counselor or mentor based on their profile and expertise. While we do our best to accommodate these requests, availability may vary. If your preferred counselor is not available, we'll match you with someone who has similar expertise and experience.
