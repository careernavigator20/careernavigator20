Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your customer support hours?

Our customer support team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM EST, and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM EST. We are closed on Sundays and major holidays.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend using our live chat feature during business hours for immediate assistance.

Can I schedule a demo of your platform?

Yes, we offer free demos of our platform for both potential students and counselors. You can request a demo through the contact form on this page or by emailing demo@careerconnect.com with your preferred date and time.
